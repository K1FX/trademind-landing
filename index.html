FAQ

Common questions.

Is TradeMind really free to start?
Yes. The free plan lets you log 20 trades per month, see basic stats, ask the AI 5 questions per day, and access the economic calendar. No credit card required. You can upgrade anytime.
What brokers does support?
TradeMind supports 500+ brokers via MetaApi including IC Markets, Pepperstone, Exness, XM, FTMO, The5ers, MyForexFunds, Funded Next, Tradeify, and many more. If your broker runs MT4 or MT5, it works.
How does the AI behavior detection work?
TradeMind analyzes patterns across all your trades. It detects revenge trading (losses followed by bigger losses same day), overtrading (too many trades in a session), bad risk management (inconsistent lot sizes), and more. It gets smarter as you add more trades.
What is the 30-day money back guarantee?
If you're not satisfied with TradeMind Pro or Elite within 30 days of subscribing, email us at support@trademind.ai and we'll refund you 100%, immediately, no questions asked.
Is my trading data safe?
Yes. Your data is stored securely on Supabase (enterprise-grade PostgreSQL). Each user's data is isolated with row-level security. We never sell or share your trading data with anyone.
How does the affiliate program work?
You earn 30% of every payment made by traders you refer — for as long as they stay subscribed. That means if you refer someone to Pro ($29/mo), you earn $8.70 every month. Payouts via PayPal or bank transfer monthly.
